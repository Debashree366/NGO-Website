ABOUT WeCare

Offices in over 30 countries

Led by Mr. A, the Chairman, WeCare Group is a torchbearer of trusteeship and responsible business ethos. Based on the philosophy of ‘Doing Well and Doing Good’, the Group continues to create long-term value for its stakeholders and the community at large.

Driven by its core values, WeCare Group steadfastly pursues inclusive growth, while adhering to ethical and values-driven practices.

Each social initiative nurtured by WeCare Foundation, addresses one of the three focus areas :

WeCare Swasthya: WeCare Healthcare and Nutrition

WeCare School of Leadership: Education Leadership and Women & Youth Empowerment

WeCare Sarvajal: Access to Safe Drinking Water
