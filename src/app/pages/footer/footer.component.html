About us

WeCare Foundation, the philanthropic arm of the WeCare Group develops innovative solutions to resolve issues that are critical roadblocks towards unlocking India’s economic potential.

Its sustainable development programmes in healthcare & nutrition, education and safe drinking water across 25 Indian states, largely in partnership with the central and state governments have impacted over 100 Million lives till date.
